My friend Jordan and I created a narrative comedy-fiction podcast about a hat store in the 1800's that is being recorded via-time travel. I recognize that this is a very silly thing, and perhaps not the most 'professional' piece of production, but I wanted to include it because it is a much better example of my skillset than just commercial spots.

Jordan plays the main character, I perform all the ancillary voices, and I completely produced this first episode. This podcast is appropriate for all ages.

In the course of my radio work currently, I have not had as much occasion to produce longer-form dynamic content, because our stations' formats don't really call for it. However, I am very passionate about sound design and more involved production, so I wanted to demonstrate my versatility in some way.
